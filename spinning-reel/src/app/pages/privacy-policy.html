Privacy Policy

Sept 2022

Introduction

This Privacy Policy is for Spinning Reel. This policy applies to all persons that provide their personal information to Spinning Reel regardless of how their Personal Information is collected.

Should you have any queries regarding the security or integrity of your Personal Information, please don’t hesitate to contact us on info@spinningreel.co.za.

Spinning Reel respects your privacy and the importance of ensuring that your Personal Information is stored and is used in a manner that complies with local data privacy laws.

‍

What is Personal Information?

Personal Information includes information that assists Spinning Reel or any person in identifying you. This could be, for example, your name or surname or it could be information about how to contact you such as your e-mail address.

Personal Information does not include information that is collected for statistical purposes and which cannot be linked to you or used to identify you.

‍

What kind of Personal Information does Spinning Reel collect?

Spinning Reel collects Personal Information about your identity and how to contact you.

‍

How does Spinning Reel collect your Personal Information?

Your Personal Information could be collected through the following interactions:

  • Telephonic interactions with Spinning Reel staff;
  • Electronic interactions (E-mail/messaging/social media) with the Spinning Reel staff;
  • By providing your Personal Information on the Spinning Reel website.

‍

What does Spinning Reel do with your Personal Information?

Spinning Reel uses your Personal Information to make accommodation bookings, including any communication related to your booking.

‍

How do I access the Personal Information that Spinning Reel has about me or my child(ren)?

Any Personal Information held by Spinning Reel can be accessed by sending a request for your personal information to info@spinningreel.co.za.

‍

How do I update my Personal Information held by Spinning Reel?

Personal Information can be updated by e-mailing info@spinningreel.co.za

‍

How do I ensure that Spinning Reel deletes my Personal Information that I do not wish for Rivers to keep regarding my child(ren) or myself?

Requests for Personal Information to be deleted can be sent to info@spinningreel.co.za.

‍

Changes to this policy

Changes may apply to this policy from time to time.

‍

Acceptance of this policy

By providing us with your Personal Information through any of the above-mentioned means of collection, you consent to Spinning Reel using your Personal Information in the manner described herein.
